EG 10: What's Your Content?

Our group has decided to do a podcast. A talk about our high school journey , our memories, and our thoughts about going back to face to face classes.

Livestream Plan

Streaming Set-up:

Streaming Software: OBS Studio

Streaming Platform: Youtube

Equipment each member of the group will need:

- Stable internet connection

- A device to use with camera

- A microphone (any kind)

Kind of Stream:

Podcast

Topic/s:

- Highschool Journey

- Memorable Moments of your/our highschool life

- Thoughts about going back in Face to Face classes

Goal/s:

To pick topics whereas we can interact with the possible topics where they can also share their experiences and their thoughts. We would like for our audience to relate and interact with us through the discussion.

Target Audience:

Our target audience are High School students, College students, and University Graduates. They are our targeted audience because they all have gathered memories and experiences that are possibly related to our topic.
